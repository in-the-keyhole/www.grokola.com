Features

GrokOla supports development team 'tribal knowledge' transfer and retention. It pairs an easy-to-use Wiki platform with a question and answer workflow that connects users with subject matter experts.

Ask Questions to Experts

Users can ask detailed development questions to subject matter experts, GrokStars, and receive an answer in less than 24 hours.

No knowledge slips through the cracks! Once an answer is accepted by the user, it is made available by GrokOla search to all users of your private instance.

A Secure, Private Instance

Your development team is set up with its own secure, private instance to ensure that your 'Tribal Knowledge' can be viewed by those with the rights to access.

Educational Content Updated Daily

With GrokOla, you receive free educational content created by the Keyhole Labs team. This can include self-paced courses, language or tool primers (like JavaScript or Node.js), tricky programming concepts, and development presentations.

Code content (Groks) for popular technologies (Java, JavaScript, C#, Git, etc.) are also updated on a daily basis. This content is automatically imported into your private GrokOla instance.

Code-Sensitive Wiki & Lucene-Backed Search

Code solutions are documented using simple markdown syntax and language-sensitive editors. Your team can utilize this wiki for any information users may need - on-boarding documents & procedures, proprietary code walkthroughs, any 'Tribal Knowledge' your team needs to retain, etc.

All GrokOla content in your private instance is searchable through a powerful Lucene search engine.

Wiki Image Support & Presentation Mode

Wiki content is easily customizable. Images and screenshots can easily be uploaded and added, and content can be displayed in a full screen presentation format at the touch of a button.

Analytics Dashboard

Questions asked - and on what topics they are asked - are displayed in a graphical dashboard. This can help organizations determine what technologies its development teams are struggling with.
