Features

GrokOla supports development team 'tribal knowledge' transfer and retention.

It is an easy-to-use Wiki platform for software development knowledge. It integrates a question and answer workflow that pairs users with subject matter experts. Here are some features of GrokOla:

Code-Sensitive Wiki

Code solutions are documented using simple markdown syntax. Also, editors are language-sensitive.

Wiki Image Support

Images and screenshots can easily be uploaded and added to GrokOla's Wiki content.

Patented Question/Answer Workflow

With GrokOla, users can ask questions to subject matter experts, GrokStars. Questions won't fall through the cracks as they are communicated to teams at every step. The status - when and who is answering - of questions are tracked through email notifications. Answers to questions are available to everyone.

Analytics Dashboard

Questions asked - and on what topics they are asked - are displayed in a graphical dashboard. This can help organizations determine what technologies its development teams are struggling with.

Lucene-Backed Search

All GrokOla content is searchable through a powerful Lucene search engine. Your team's 'Tribal Knowledge' stays at your team's fingertips for future use.
