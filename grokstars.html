Grokstars

One of the great things about GrokOla is easy access to the answers of your development questions. If you don't know something, all you have to do is enter in your question, and ask the experts from your team. But sometimes, your development team experts might not know the answer. That's okay - no one can know it all! That's when our GrokStars come in.

With your GrokOla membership, you can ask your questions to GrokStar subject matter experts.



It still works the same as when you're asking your experts using our patented workflow - but you're getting external support as part of your GrokOla membership.
