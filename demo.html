Active Keyhole Software clients automatically receive a free, private instance of GrokOla. If an engagement ends, that client can continue using GrokOla per the monthly pricing, or export the Groks (answers) and content.

Both hosted and installed options are available. For pricing details, please contact Keyhole Labs directly to discuss your needs.
